Introduction to Combinators

Combinators allow you style particular elements depending on their relationship with other elements.

Sibling Combinators

In the following list, any list item that comes after another list item is styled.

The first list item is not styled because it is the reference point.

By using it as a reference point, any list item that comes after it is styled.

  1. I am the first item.
    • I am an item in an unordered list.
  2. I am the second item.
  3. I am the third item.
