Introduction to Combinators

Combinators allow you style particular elements depending on their relationship with other elements.

Direct Child Combinators

In the following list, all the items directly under the ordered list are styled.

The direct child combinator styles only the child of a parent within one level of depth.

This means that if a list item is a descendant of the ordered list but they are separated by other elements, these list items will not be styled.

The only unstyled list item is not colored because it is within the unordered list within the ordered list. It is not a direct child.
